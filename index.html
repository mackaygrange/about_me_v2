My Personal Interests

Hello, my Name is Mackay Grange. Welcome to my webpage! I am a Software Engineering Major at UVU. I am currently a senior and I am excited to graduate.

One of my biggest interests and favorite past time is working on personal coding projects. I love to come up with stupid ideas and reinvent the wheel. My wheels do not work very well, but they are my wheels. My most recent project consisted of me constructing a 3D modeling engine in C, using only the SDL library as a dependancy. I really enjoyed learning about quaternions for 3D rotations, and the linear algebra involved in translating and projecting 3D space.

I also really enjoy reading books, spending time with my wife, skiing, hanging out with my four cats, and playing Magic the Gathering with my friends. I recently moved to Payson, Utah in order to attend UVU. Previously I was attending Utah State University, majoring in Computer Science.
